## Ingredients
- 1 cup water
- 1/2 cup plain flour
- 1 cup rice flour
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp chilli powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets
- 2 green chillies, finely chopped
- a handful of curry leaves
- 3 tbsp unsweetened plant yoghurt

## Method
1. Blanch cauliflower briefly in hot water, then rinse and drain well.
2. Mix plain flour, rice flour, garam masala, black pepper, chilli powder, salt, and water to make a thick batter.
3. Coat cauliflower in the batter and deep fry until golden and crispy.
4. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, garlic, green chillies, and curry leaves.
5. Stir in chilli powder and garam masala, then add the plant yoghurt, cooking until thick.
6. Toss the fried cauliflower in the spicy mixture over high heat for 2–3 minutes.
7. Serve hot as a starter or snack.

## Notes
- Ensure the batter is thick for better coating.
- Adjust spice levels according to taste.
- For a gluten-free option, use a gluten-free flour blend.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container; reheat in the oven for crispiness.
