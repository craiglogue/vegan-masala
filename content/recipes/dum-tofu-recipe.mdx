## Ingredients
- oil
- onion
- cloves
- cardamom
- cinnamon stick
- ginger
- garlic
- green chillies
- unsweetened plant-based yoghurt
- coriander powder
- cumin powder
- turmeric powder
- red chilli powder
- salt
- garam masala
- black pepper powder
- firm tofu cubes
- plant-based cream
- crushed black peppercorn
- mint leaves
- water
- coriander leaves

## Method
1. Heat oil in a pan.
2. Sauté the onion until golden brown, then blend it into a paste.
3. Add cloves, cardamom, cinnamon stick, and the onion paste to the pan.
4. Sauté the mixture before adding ginger, garlic, and green chillies.
5. Incorporate plant-based yoghurt along with cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chilli powders, salt, and garam masala, mixing well.
6. Add black pepper powder and stir through.
7. Introduce the tofu cubes and gently sauté.
8. Drizzle plant-based cream over the mixture.
9. Pour in water to create a gravy, then stir in crushed black peppercorn.
10. Seal the pan with foil and a lid, allowing it to cook.
11. Once cooked, remove the cover and add mint and coriander leaves.
12. Stir well and serve hot.

## Notes
- For a spicier dish, increase the amount of green chillies or red chilli powder.
- Store leftovers in the fridge for up to three days and reheat gently on the stove.
