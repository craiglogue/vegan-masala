## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- curry powder
- turmeric
- 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- lime zest

## Method
1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
2. Mix oil, spices, and turmeric in a large bowl.
3. Add cauliflower and toss to coat evenly.
4. Spread on a baking tray in a single layer.
5. Roast for 10–15 minutes until golden and tender.
6. Finish with grated ginger and lime zest before serving.
