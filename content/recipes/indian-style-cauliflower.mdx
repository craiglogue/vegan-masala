ingredients:
  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "1 tsp coriander seeds"
  - "1 tsp cumin seeds"
  - "1 tsp curry powder"
  - "1 tsp turmeric"
  - "1 large cauliflower, cut into florets"
  - "Salt and black pepper"
  - "1 tsp grated ginger"
  - "1 tsp lime zest"

instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 220°C / 450°F."
  - "Mix oil, spices and turmeric in a large bowl."
  - "Add cauliflower and toss to coat evenly."
  - "Spread on a baking tray in a single layer."
  - "Roast for 10–15 minutes until golden and tender."
  - "Finish with grated ginger and lime zest before serving."

## Indian-Style Cauliflower

A simple roasted side dish that pairs perfectly with curries, rice bowls or flatbreads.

### Tips
- Add chilli flakes for heat.
- Great served with vegan yogurt or chutney.
- Also works in wraps and grain bowls.
