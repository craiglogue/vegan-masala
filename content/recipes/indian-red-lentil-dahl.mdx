## Ingredients
- coconut oil
- onions, chopped
- garlic cloves, minced
- grated ginger
- turmeric
- ground coriander
- ground cumin
- paprika
- garam masala
- red lentils, rinsed
- vegetable broth
- coconut milk
- strained or chopped tomatoes (optional)
- salt and pepper to taste
- coconut sugar (optional)
- lime or lemon juice
- fresh coriander, to serve

## Method
1. Heat coconut oil in a saucepan and sauté onions until soft.
2. Add garlic and ginger; cook until fragrant.
3. Stir in spices and cook briefly to release their aroma.
4. Add rinsed lentils and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer and cook for 8–10 minutes.
5. Stir in coconut milk and tomatoes (if using). Simmer until lentils are soft.
6. Adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, sugar and lime.
7. Serve with rice and fresh coriander.

## Notes
- For extra creaminess, use full-fat coconut milk.
- Storage: Keeps well in the fridge for up to 5 days; can be frozen.
