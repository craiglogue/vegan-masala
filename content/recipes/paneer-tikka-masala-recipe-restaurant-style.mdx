## Ingredients
- firm tofu, cubed
- bell pepper, diced
- onion, layered
- thick plant yoghurt
- ginger garlic paste
- lemon juice
- Kashmiri chilli powder
- garam masala
- turmeric
- chickpea flour
- salt to taste
- oil for cooking
- onions for gravy
- tomatoes, pureed
- coriander powder
- cumin powder
- chilli powder
- dried fenugreek leaves
- water
- plant cream
- sugar (optional)
- fresh coriander for garnish

## Method
1. Mix plant yoghurt, spices, lemon juice, and chickpea flour to create the marinade.
2. Add tofu, onion, and bell pepper; marinate for 1 hour.
3. Bake at 200°C for 15–20 minutes or pan-fry until lightly charred.
4. Blend onions into a paste and tomatoes into a puree.
5. Heat oil and cook the onion paste until golden.
6. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté briefly.
7. Stir in tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
8. Add dry spices and cook until fragrant.
9. Lower heat and carefully stir in plant yoghurt.
10. Add water and simmer until the gravy thickens.
11. Incorporate plant cream and dried fenugreek leaves.
12. Gently add grilled tofu tikka pieces and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
13. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

## Notes
- Adjust spice levels to taste.
- Serve with rice or naan.
