## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp oil
- bay leaves
- 2 green cardamom pods
- cloves
- 1 small cinnamon stick
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 1 medium tomato, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 1 green chilli, finely chopped
- 1 cup water
- 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
- turmeric
- ground cumin
- 1 tsp salt
- sugar (optional)
- dry soy curls
- 3 tbsp unsweetened plant yoghurt
- plant milk
- dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
- 1 tsp garam masala

## Method
1. Turn Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat oil or broth.
2. Add bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon; cook until fragrant.
3. Add onion and sauté until translucent.
4. Blend tomato, tomato paste, garlic, ginger, green chilli, and water.
5. Add blended mixture and ground spices to the pot.
6. Add soy curls, yoghurt, salt, and sugar. Stir well.
7. Seal and pressure cook for 15 minutes. Allow for natural release.
8. Stir in plant milk and simmer on sauté mode until creamy.
9. Incorporate fenugreek leaves and garam masala.
10. Garnish with coriander and serve with rice or flatbread.
