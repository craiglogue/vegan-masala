## Ingredients
- spinach
- potatoes
- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- ginger
- cumin seeds
- coriander powder
- garam masala
- turmeric
- chilli powder
- vegan butter or oil
- salt
- water

## Method
1. Heat vegan butter or oil in a pan; add cumin seeds and cook until fragrant.
2. Sauté onions until golden, then add ginger and garlic; cook for 1-2 minutes.
3. Stir in chopped tomatoes and spices (coriander, garam masala, turmeric, chilli powder), cooking until tomatoes soften.
4. Add diced potatoes, stir to coat in spices, then add water; cover and cook until potatoes are tender.
5. Once potatoes are cooked, mix in the spinach and cook until wilted; season with salt.

## Notes
- For creaminess, consider adding coconut cream towards the end.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to three days.
- Adjust spice levels based on your preference; add more chilli for extra heat.
