## Ingredients
- cauliflower florets
- potatoes, diced
- oil
- mustard seeds
- cumin seeds
- ginger, grated
- green chillies, slit
- turmeric powder
- coriander powder
- chilli powder
- salt
- water
- fresh coriander, chopped

## Method
1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard and cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add grated ginger and slit green chillies.
2. Stir in turmeric, coriander, and chilli powder, and sauté for a minute.
3. Add diced potatoes and cauliflower florets. Mix well to coat the vegetables with spices.
4. Pour in water, add salt, and cover the pan. Cook on medium heat until the vegetables are tender.
5. Once cooked, garnish with fresh coriander before serving.

## Notes
- For extra creaminess, add a splash of coconut cream towards the end.
- Adjust spice levels by varying the number of green chillies.
- Serve with rice or naan for a complete meal.
