## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- mixed seasonal vegetables, chopped
- 200 g firm tofu, cubed
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 1 green chilli, finely chopped
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped

## Method
1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Sauté until they sizzle.
2. Add chopped onion and green chilli, cooking until the onion is translucent.
3. Stir in minced garlic and sauté for a couple of minutes until fragrant.
4. Add the mixed vegetables, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. Add tofu cubes and a splash of water if necessary. Cover the pan and let it simmer until the vegetables are tender.
6. Finish with lemon juice and chopped coriander before serving.

## Notes
- Feel free to swap seasonal vegetables based on availability.
- Tofu is a great substitute for paneer for added protein.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
- Adjust spices to your preference for a milder or more intense flavour.
