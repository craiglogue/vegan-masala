ingredients:
  - "1 tsp oil"
  - "¼ tsp cumin seeds"
  - "¼ tsp coriander seeds"
  - "¼ cup red onion, chopped"
  - "2 tbsp cashews"
  - "½ green chilli, finely chopped"
  - "¾ tsp turmeric"
  - "½ tsp ground cumin"
  - "½ tsp ground coriander"
  - "¼ tsp cayenne (to taste)"
  - "2 tsp minced ginger"
  - "½ tsp garlic powder"
  - "2 cups potatoes, cubed"
  - "½ tsp salt"
  - "½ tsp chaat masala or amchur"
  - "⅓ cup water"
  - "¼ cup green peas"
  - "2 tbsp chopped coriander"
  - "2 tbsp chopped mint"
  - "1 tsp lemon juice"
  - "1 sheet vegan puff pastry"
  - "Optional greens or chutney for serving"

instructions:
  - "Heat oil and toast cumin and coriander seeds until fragrant."
  - "Add onion, chilli and cashews and cook until softened."
  - "Stir in spices, ginger and garlic powder."
  - "Add potatoes, salt and water and cook until tender."
  - "Mash some potatoes for texture, then mix in peas, herbs and lemon juice."
  - "Let filling cool slightly."
  - "Roll puff pastry to double size and place filling on one half."
  - "Fold pastry over, seal edges and cut steam vents."
  - "Bake at 200°C / 400°F for 18–22 minutes until golden."
  - "Cool slightly before slicing and serving."

## About this recipe

This samosa pie is basically a **giant baked samosa**.  
It has the same warming spices and comforting potato filling but is much easier than folding individual samosas.

### Perfect for
- Party food  
- Meal prep  
- Picnics  
- Freezer meals  

### Serving ideas
Serve with:
- Mango chutney  
- Tamarind chutney  
- Mint chutney  

### Tips

- Let the filling cool before assembling to keep the pastry crisp.
- Add chopped spinach or carrots for extra veg.
- Freeze baked slices for quick future meals.
