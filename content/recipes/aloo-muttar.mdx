## Ingredients
- potatoes, peeled and cubed
- green peas, fresh or frozen
- onion, finely chopped
- tomato, chopped
- garlic, minced
- ginger, grated
- cumin seeds
- turmeric powder
- coriander powder
- garam masala
- green chilli, slit
- oil
- salt
- coriander leaves, for garnish

## Method
1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and allow them to sizzle.
2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
3. Stir in garlic, ginger, and green chilli, cooking for a minute until fragrant.
4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until softened.
5. Mix in turmeric, coriander powder, and salt, and cook for another minute.
6. Add cubed potatoes and stir to coat in spices. Pour in water, cover, and simmer until potatoes are tender.
7. Stir in green peas and garam masala, cooking for a few more minutes until peas are heated through.
8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

## Notes
- Adjust the green chilli to control the spice level.
- Frozen peas can be added directly without thawing.
- Serve with rice or flatbreads for a delicious meal.
