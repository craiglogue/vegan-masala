## Ingredients
- dried chickpeas
- water for cooking chickpeas
- onion
- green chillies
- garlic cloves
- ginger
- neutral oil
- tomato (pureed)
- spinach, chopped
- water for gravy
- turmeric
- chilli powder
- coriander powder
- cumin powder
- garam masala
- amchur (dry mango powder)
- dried fenugreek leaves
- salt

## Method
1. Soak chickpeas overnight, then pressure cook until soft and tender.
2. Blend onion, garlic, ginger, and green chillies into a paste.
3. Heat oil and sauté whole spices until fragrant.
4. Add onion paste and cook until lightly golden.
5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
6. Stir in turmeric, chilli powder, cumin, and coriander.
7. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted.
8. Add cooked chickpeas and water, then simmer until gravy thickens.
9. Mash a few chickpeas to thicken the curry.
10. Finish with garam masala, fenugreek leaves, and amchur.
11. Serve hot with rice or flatbreads.
