  - "Stir in tamarind or lemon juice and sugar."
  - "Taste and adjust salt and acidity."
  - "Garnish with chopped coriander and serve."

## About this recipe

Whole moong dal has a deep, nutty flavour and holds its shape beautifully when pressure cooked.  
This is a simple everyday dal that pairs perfectly with rice or roti.

### Serving ideas
- Steamed basmati rice  
- Jeera rice  
- Warm roti or chapati  

### Tips
- For richer flavour, finish with a garlic tadka in hot oil.
- This dal thickens as it cools — add water when reheating.
