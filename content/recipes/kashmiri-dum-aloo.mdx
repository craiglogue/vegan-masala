## Ingredients
- 3 medium potatoes, cubed
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp chilli powder
- turmeric
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tin coconut milk
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp garam masala
- cayenne
- 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
- almonds
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- sugar

## Method
1. Blend soaked almonds, ginger, garam masala, cayenne, coriander, sugar and a little water into a smooth paste.
2. Toss baby potatoes with oil, chilli powder, turmeric and salt.
3. Roast at 200°C for about 30 minutes until fork tender.
4. Bring the spice paste to a simmer in a saucepan.
5. Add half the coconut milk and the roasted potatoes.
6. Cover and simmer on low for 15–20 minutes.
7. Stir in remaining coconut milk, lemon juice and herbs.
8. Adjust salt and serve hot.

## Notes
- For richer flavour, roast the potatoes until golden brown.
- You can use more or less chilli powder depending on desired heat.
