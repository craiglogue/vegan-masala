## Ingredients
- 200 g firm tofu, cubed
- 200 g spinach
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 green chillies, finely chopped
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 1 medium tomato, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- cashews
- 1 tsp garam masala
- dried fenugreek leaves
- 1 cup water
- 1 tsp salt
- whole spices (optional): cumin seeds, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves
- 100 ml oat cream

## Method
1. Sauté spinach, green chillies and cashews until wilted, then blend into a smooth puree.
2. Heat oil and sauté whole spices if using.
3. Cook onion until soft and lightly golden.
4. Add ginger garlic paste and cook briefly.
5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and jammy.
6. Add garam masala and cook until fragrant.
7. Pour in water and simmer until onions are completely soft.
8. Stir in spinach puree and simmer gently.
9. Add tofu cubes and cook for 3–5 minutes.
10. Finish with dried fenugreek leaves and optional plant cream.
11. Serve with roti, naan or basmati rice.
