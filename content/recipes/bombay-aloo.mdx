## Ingredients
- 3 medium potatoes, cubed
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp chilli powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 2 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Method
1. Boil the potatoes until tender, then peel and cube them.
2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and wait for them to crackle.
3. Stir in cumin seeds, then add the boiled potatoes.
4. Sprinkle turmeric, chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt over the potatoes.
5. Gently mix to coat the potatoes in the spices, cooking for about 5-7 minutes.
6. Finish with fresh coriander and a squeeze of lemon juice before serving.

## Notes
- Feel free to add green peas for extra colour and nutrition.
- Adjust the spice levels according to your taste.
- Great served with rice or flatbreads.
- Leftovers can be stored in the fridge for up to 3 days and reheated in a pan.
