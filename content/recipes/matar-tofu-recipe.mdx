## Ingredients
- firm tofu, cubed
- green peas
- oil
- cumin seeds
- onion, finely chopped
- garlic, minced
- grated ginger
- blended tomatoes or passata
- ground coriander
- garam masala
- turmeric
- chilli powder
- salt
- water
- fresh coriander for garnish

## Method
1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds until they begin to sizzle.
2. Add onion and cook until golden and soft.
3. Stir in garlic and ginger, cooking for 1 minute.
4. Add blended tomatoes and cook until thick and oil separates.
5. Mix in turmeric, coriander, chilli powder, and salt.
6. Add tofu cubes and peas with water.
7. Simmer for 10–12 minutes until curry thickens.
8. Finish with garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander.
9. Serve hot with rice or flatbreads.

## Notes
- Adjust chilli powder to taste for desired heat.
- Fresh or frozen peas work well for this dish.
