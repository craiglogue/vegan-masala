## Ingredients
- 200 g mushrooms, sliced
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 200 ml tomato puree
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 small cinnamon stick
- green cardamoms
- cloves
- turmeric
- 1 tsp chilli powder
- 2 tsp coriander powder
- 1 cup water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp garam masala
- dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
- 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped

## Method
1. Blend onion, ginger and garlic into a smooth paste.
2. Blend tomatoes into a smooth puree.
3. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom and cloves.
4. Add onion paste and sauté until golden and thick.
5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the masala.
6. Stir in turmeric, chilli powder and coriander powder.
7. Add mushrooms and mix well.
8. Pour in water and season with salt.
9. Cover and simmer until mushrooms are tender.
10. Finish with garam masala, dried fenugreek leaves and fresh coriander.
11. Serve hot with rice or flatbreads.

## Notes
- Add more water if the curry becomes too thick.
- Adjust the spice levels by increasing or decreasing chilli powder.
