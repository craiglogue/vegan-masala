ingredients:
  - "1 tbsp oil"
  - "2 green cardamom pods"
  - "1 medium onion, sliced"
  - "1 inch ginger"
  - "5–6 garlic cloves"
  - "2 cups tomatoes, chopped"
  - "15 cashews"
  - "1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)"
  - "1 tsp sugar or jaggery (optional)"
  - "1 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder or paprika"
  - "1 tsp garam masala"
  - "1 tsp ground coriander"
  - "1 cup water (for blending)"
  - "2 tbsp vegan butter"
  - "1 bay leaf (optional)"
  - "1 small cinnamon stick (optional)"
  - "3 cloves (optional)"
  - "250–300g firm tofu, cubed"
  - "½ tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek)"
  - "3–4 tbsp plant cream or coconut cream"
  - "Fresh coriander to garnish"

instructions:
  - "Sauté cardamom, onion, ginger and garlic in oil until the onion softens."
  - "Add tomatoes, cashews and salt and cook until the tomatoes break down."
  - "Stir in chilli powder, coriander and garam masala and cook until fragrant."
  - "Blend the mixture with water until completely smooth."
  - "Melt vegan butter in the pan with bay leaf, cinnamon and cloves if using."
  - "Pour the blended sauce back into the pan and simmer until slightly thick."
  - "Add tofu cubes and simmer gently for a few minutes."
  - "Stir in sugar, kasuri methi and plant cream and cook briefly."
  - "Garnish with fresh coriander and serve."

## About this recipe

This vegan butter masala is inspired by the famous restaurant classic.  
Cashews and tomatoes create a silky, luxurious sauce while tofu absorbs all the flavour beautifully.

### Serving ideas
- Basmati rice or jeera rice  
- Naan or roti  
- Pickled onions and fresh coriander  

### Tips

- Blend the sauce really smooth for the best restaurant texture.
- Use coconut cream for a richer curry.
- This tastes even better the next day.
