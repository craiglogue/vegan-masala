A well-stocked pantry makes vegan Indian cooking much easier. Once you have a few key ingredients on hand, you can cook a wide range of curries, dals, and vegetable dishes without much planning.

## Essential Spices

A small spice collection goes a long way in vegan Indian cooking.

Once you have these basics, cooking vegan Indian food becomes much easier.
